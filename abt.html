Cristian Hernandez-Blick is an MA student in the graduate program of Science and Technology Studies at the University of British Columbia. His research interests involve philosophical issues related to biological agency and autonomy, synthetic morphology, biological robotics, autopoiesis and the relationship between organisms, artifacts, and teleology. He is currently Head of Program at Interspecies Internet, an organization working to promote and advance the study of nonhuman animal communication, cognition, behaviour, and welfare. He also creates abstract digital illustrations, works occasionally on front-end web design, and previously co-founded a small, alt-comics press named DDOOGG.
