Cristian Hernandez-Blick is an MA student in the graduate program of Science and Technology Studies at the University of British Columbia. His research interests include: philosophical issues related to advances in synthetic developmental biology and biological computing, the world history of eugenics, self-organizing systems and autopoietic theory, and the relationship between organisms, artifacts, and notions of teleology. He also creates abstract digital illustrations, works occasionally on front-end web design, and previously co-founded a small, alt-comics press named DDOOGG.
